About Me

Hello! I am a final-year B.A.M.S. (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery) student with a deep passion for the ancient science of healing and wellness.

I believe in a holistic approach to health, integrating traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern understanding to promote balance in mind, body, and spirit. My goal is to serve the community by making this profound knowledge accessible and applicable to everyday life.
